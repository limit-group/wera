Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 30/1/2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Wera. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when using our platform.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email, and any other necessary details.
  • Job Postings & Applications: Employers and job seekers provide information such as resumes, job descriptions, and communications.
  • Usage Data: We collect data on how you interact with our platform to improve our services.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and manage our platform's features.
  • To facilitate job matching between employers and job seekers.
  • To enhance security, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.
  • To offer customer support and improve user experience.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share data with:

  • Employers and Job Seekers: To facilitate hiring and contracting.
  • Service Providers: To assist with platform operations.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect user safety.

5. Data Security

We implement strong security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, but no system is entirely secure. Use caution when sharing sensitive information.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
  • Request data portability or restriction of processing.

7. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to improve functionality and analyze user activity. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the platform after updates means you accept the changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@wera.co.ke.
